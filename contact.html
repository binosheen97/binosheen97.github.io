Frequently Asked Questions

Are your calculators really free?

Yes! All our calculators are 100% free to use with no hidden charges, subscriptions, or registrations required.

Do I need to create an account?

No! You can use all our calculators instantly without creating an account or providing any personal information.

Do you store my calculation data?

No. All calculations happen directly in your web browser. We don't collect, store, or transmit your calculation data to our servers.

Can I suggest a new calculator?

Absolutely! We love hearing from users. Visit our GitHub page or use the feedback link above to suggest new calculators.

I found a bug. How do I report it?

Thank you! Please use the "Report Bug" link above or create an issue on our GitHub repository with details about the bug.

Can I use your calculators on mobile?

Yes! All our calculators are fully responsive and work perfectly on smartphones and tablets.

Are your calculations accurate?

Yes! All our calculators use tested and verified algorithms. However, for critical decisions, always consult a professional.

Can I embed your calculators on my website?

Please contact us through GitHub to discuss embedding options and proper attribution requirements.

Do you have an API?

Not currently, but if there's enough interest, we may consider it. Let us know if you'd use one!

How often do you add new calculators?

We're constantly working on new tools! Follow our GitHub to stay updated on new releases.

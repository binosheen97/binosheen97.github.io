📋 Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 10, 2026

Welcome to Free Online Calculators. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website https://binosheen97.github.io/.

Important: We are committed to protecting your privacy. We do NOT collect any personal information that can identify you individually. Our calculators work entirely in your browser.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Automatically Collected Information

When you visit our website, we automatically collect certain information about your device and browsing behavior through:

  • Google Analytics: We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our site. This includes:
    • Browser type and version
    • Operating system
    • Pages visited and time spent
    • Country and city (approximate location)
    • IP address (anonymized)
  • Cookies: Small text files stored on your device to enhance your experience

1.2 Information You Provide

Our calculators (Age Calculator, BMI Calculator, etc.) process data you enter (dates, weight, height, etc.). This information:

  • ✅ Is processed entirely in your browser
  • ✅ Is NEVER sent to our servers
  • ✅ Is NOT stored or saved anywhere
  • ✅ Disappears when you close or refresh the page

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the automatically collected information to:

  • Understand how visitors use our calculators
  • Improve our website and user experience
  • Analyze traffic patterns and popular content
  • Detect and prevent technical issues

3. Third-Party Services

3.1 Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to analyze website traffic. Google Analytics uses cookies to collect information. You can opt-out of Google Analytics by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

Read Google's Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

3.2 Google AdSense

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements on our website. Google and its partners use cookies to:

  • Serve ads based on your prior visits to our website or other websites
  • Measure ad performance and relevance
  • Provide personalized advertising experiences

You can opt-out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.

Read Google AdSense Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads

3.3 GitHub Pages

Our website is hosted on GitHub Pages. GitHub may collect technical information about visitors. Read GitHub's Privacy Statement: https://docs.github.com/en/site-policy/privacy-policies/github-privacy-statement

4. Cookies

We use cookies to enhance your experience. Types of cookies we use:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for the website to function properly
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how you use our site (Google Analytics)
  • Advertising Cookies: Used to show relevant ads (Google AdSense)

You can control cookies through your browser settings. However, disabling cookies may affect website functionality.

5. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. Since we don't collect personal information through our calculators, there's minimal risk to your data.

6. Children's Privacy

Our website is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Our calculators are educational tools that process data locally in your browser.

7. Your Rights (GDPR & CCPA)

If you are located in the European Union or California, you have certain rights regarding your data:

  • Right to Access: Request information about data we collect
  • Right to Deletion: Request deletion of your data
  • Right to Opt-Out: Opt-out of analytics and advertising cookies
  • Right to Non-Discrimination: We will not discriminate if you exercise your rights

8. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. Google Analytics and AdSense operate globally and may process data in various countries.

9. Do Not Track

Some browsers include a "Do Not Track" (DNT) feature. Currently, there is no industry standard for responding to DNT signals. We do not track users across different websites.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

11. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

  • Website: https://binosheen97.github.io/
  • GitHub: @binosheen97
